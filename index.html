FAQ

What if my size changes?

That's normal. Waist beads are worn for awareness; they help you keep track of your weight changes.

Do you deliver?

Yes. We deliver locally in Lusaka and internationally.

How do I measure my waist?
  1. Decide where you want them to sit
  2. Stand naturally
  3. Measure around that area — use a soft measuring tape, wrap it snugly, not tightly
  4. No tape? Use a string, mark it, then measure with a ruler
  5. Confirm your measurement — measure twice to be sure
